<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-176">**家長管理層級**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-176">**Parental Management Levels**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-177">DVD 光碟的所有或部分都可使用家長管理層級進行編碼， (>PROCMONTRACE.PML) 從一到八的編號。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-177">All or part of a DVD disc can be encoded with a Parental Management Level (PML) numbered from one to eight.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-178">八是最嚴格的 (成人) ，而其中一個是最不嚴格的 () 的年齡。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-178">Eight is the most restrictive level (adults only) and one is the least restrictive (all ages).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-179">其構想是防止兒童在沒有家長同意的情況下監看成人內容，同時允許成人監看兒童安全的內容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-179">The idea is to prevent children from watching adult content without parental consent, while allowing adults to watch child-safe content.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-180">在美國和加拿大，這些層級會對應到 MPAA (G、PG、PG-13、NC-17) 的評等系統，但在其他國家或地區則不會發生這種情況。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-180">In the United States and Canada, the levels map to the rating system of the MPAA (G, PG, PG-13, NC-17), but this is not the case in other countries or regions.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-181">因為章節可以邏輯方式存在於家長監護內，所以標題中可能有兩個相同章節的版本，每個都指派不同的 >PROCMONTRACE.PML 和不同的父區塊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-181">Because chapters can exist logically within a parental block, there may be two versions of the same chapter in a title, each assigned a different PML and in a different parental block.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-182">例如，登入並播放光碟的子系會看到一版的第3章，而登入的成人會看到不同的版本（假設應用程式支援 PMLs）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-182">For example, a child who logs in and plays the disc would see one version of Chapter 3, and an adult who logs in would see a different version, assuming that the application supports PMLs.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-183">標題或章節也可以包含暫時 PMLs，其內容的分級高於標題或章節的 >PROCMONTRACE.PML。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-183">A title or chapter can also contain temporary PMLs, whose content is rated higher than the PML for the title or chapter as a whole.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-184">這表示標題可能有一個以上的家長層級。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-184">This means that a title may have more than one parental level.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-185">暫存 PMLs 通常會編寫為角度區塊，因此電影中的場景可能會有兩個版本，一種是針對較年輕的檢視器，另一種是成人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-185">Temporary PMLs are generally authored as angle blocks, so that a scene in a film may have two versions, one rated for younger viewers and one for adults.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-186">播放機應用程式必須負責強制執行家長監護。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-186">It is the responsibility of the player application to enforce the parental levels.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-187">**網域**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-187">**Domains**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-188">「 *網域* 」一詞指的是 DVD 播放機的內部狀態;這並不是在光碟上撰寫的內容。網域很重要，因為某些 DVD 命令只在某些網域中有效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-188">The term *domain* refers to the internal state of a DVD player; it is not something authored on the disc. Domains are important because some DVD commands are only valid in certain domains.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-189">DirectShow 提供一種方式來查詢目前的網域，並在網域變更時收到通知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-189">DirectShow provides a way to query the current domain and to be notified when the domain changes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-190">系統會定義下列網域：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-190">The following domains are defined:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-191">**初次播放。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-191">**First Play.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-192">在此網域中，DVD 播放程式剛剛開始播放 DVD。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-192">In this domain, the DVD player has just started playing the DVD.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-193">進入第一個 Play 網域之後，播放玩家會切換到另一個網域，也就是功能表網域或標題網域，視光碟而定。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-193">After it enters the First Play domain, the player switches to another domain—either a menu domain or the title domain, depending on the disc.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-194">**影片管理員功能表。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-194">**Video Manager Menu.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-195">播放程式顯示的是 [影片管理員] 功能表，也稱為標題功能表。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-195">The player is showing the Video Manager Menu, also called the title menu.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-196">**VTS 功能表。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-196">**VTS Menu.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-197">播放程式會顯示與影片標題集相關聯的功能表，也就是根功能表或子功能表， (音訊、子圖片、角度或章節) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-197">The player is showing a menu associated with a video title set, either the root menu or a submenu (audio, sub-picture, angle, or chapter).</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-198">**標題。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-198">**Title.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-199">播放程式現正播放標題中的影片。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-199">The player is playing video in a title.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-200">**停止。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-200">**Stop.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-201">播放程式未顯示任何程式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-201">The player is not displaying anything.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-202"> (嚴格來說，DVD 規格不會將這種狀態稱為網域，但可視為一個。 ) </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-202">(Strictly speaking, the DVD specification does not call this state a domain, but it can be treated as one.)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-203">網域可以視為 DVD 播放程式所監視的狀態變數，以追蹤播放程式目前從光碟讀取的內容類型。 DVD 播放程式會使用網域，以避免對 DVD 光碟機發出無意義的命令。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-203">The domain can be thought of as a state variable that a DVD player monitors, in order to keep track of the type of content that the player is currently reading from the disc. DVD players use domains to avoid issuing meaningless commands to the DVD drive.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-204">**使用者操作控制項**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-204">**User Operation Controls**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-205">使用者操作控制項 (UOPs) 是 DVD 作者可以在任何地方插入的片片標記，以限制使用者的流覽選項。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-205">User Operation Controls (UOPs) are markers on a disc that DVD authors can insert anywhere to restrict a user's navigation options.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-206">大部分的光碟都遵循標準 UOP 限制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-206">Most discs follow standard UOP restrictions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-207">例如，大部分的光碟都不允許檢視器向前快轉，或在第一次播放網域中顯示功能表。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-207">For example, most discs do not allow the viewer to fast forward or show a menu while in First Play domain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-208">基本上，每個光碟都可以在光碟上的任何位置插入任何 UOP 命令，即使命令在目前網域內是有效的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-208">In principle, each disc can insert any UOP command at any point on the disc, even if the command would otherwise be valid within the current domain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-209">例如，您可以撰寫光碟來不允許在特定標題中快速轉送，或防止特定功能表在使用者輸入標題網域之後顯示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-209">For example, a disc may be authored to disallow fast forwarding in a certain title or to prevent a particular menu from being shown after the user enters the title domain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fdb2-210">DVD 導覽器會符合光碟中的所有這類命令，而且不允許應用程式覆寫光碟的 UOP 控制項。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fdb2-210">The DVD Navigator complies with all such commands from the disc and will not allow an application to override the disc's UOP controls.</span></span>
