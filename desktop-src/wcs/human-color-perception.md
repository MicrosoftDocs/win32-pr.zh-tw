<span data-ttu-id="67fbc-113">人們可以藉由將視力為 *棒敲* 和 *圓錐* 的視覺資料格存在，來取得人為願景。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-113">Human vision is made possible by the presence of light sensitive cells in the eye called *rods* and *cones*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-114">研究顯示，棒敲似乎不牽涉到人類的色彩認知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-114">Research has shown that the rods do not seem to be involved in the perception of color in human beings.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-115">Retina 中有三種不同的圓錐類型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-115">There are three different types of cones in the retina.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-116">每種類型的錐形都會偵測到紅色、綠色或藍色。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-116">Each type of cone detects either red, green, or blue.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-117">人們看到的所有其他色彩都是這三種色彩的混合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-117">All other colors humans see are mixtures of these three colors.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-118">比方說，當出現大約相等的紅色、綠色和藍色時，就會察覺到白色。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-118">For instance, white is perceived when approximately equal amounts of red, green, and blue are seen.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-119">當眼睛未偵測到任何紅色、綠色或藍色 (很少或沒有任何) 光線時，就會出現黑色。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-119">Black is seen when no red, green, or blue (very little or no light at all) is detected by the eye.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-120">人們看到的色彩數量也取決於光源的強度、集中程度和位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-120">The amount of color humans see also depends on the strength, concentration, and position of the light source.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="67fbc-121">光源可能對色彩的認知有深遠的影響。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67fbc-121">Lighting conditions can have a profound effect on the perception of color.</span></span>
