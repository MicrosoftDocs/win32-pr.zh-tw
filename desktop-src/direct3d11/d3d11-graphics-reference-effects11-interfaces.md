|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

| [**ID3DX11EffectVectorVariable**](id3dx11effectvectorvariable.md)<br/>                           | 向量變數介面會存取四個元件的向量。<br/>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
