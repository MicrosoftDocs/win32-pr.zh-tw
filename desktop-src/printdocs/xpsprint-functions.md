|-----------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [**StartXpsPrintJob**](/windows/desktop/api/XpsPrint/nf-xpsprint-startxpsprintjob)<br/>   | 開始將 XPS 檔串流列印到印表機。<br/>               |
