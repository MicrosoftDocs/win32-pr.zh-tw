<span data-ttu-id="ab9f2-105">Direct3D 10、Direct3D 11 和 Direct3D 12 都會使用 DXGI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ab9f2-105">DXGI is used by Direct3D 10, Direct3D 11 and Direct3D 12.</span></span>
